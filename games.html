Games I Enjoy

Elden Ring

I love Elden Ring because it rewards the player who is curious and patient. There isn't much hand-holding and the only way to learn is to explore its vast map. Every area has its own mood, backstory, atmosphere, and brings its own version of curiousity in the player.

Now, in my opinion, the best part about the game, the skill tree and weapons. You customize the character to be your own, either a sword weilding ronin or a caveman who weilds a giant club. The game is unique every single time you play it.
